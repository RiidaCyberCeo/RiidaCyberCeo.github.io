Snack & Sweet Trio

Chicken Bacon Ranch Fries

Prep 45 min | Serves 4

Ingredients: 4 potatoes, 2 cups chicken, 6 bacon slices, 1 cup cheddar, 1/2 cup ranch, salt, pepper, oil.

Instructions: Soak & dry potatoes. Fry golden, season. Layer chicken, bacon, cheese. Bake 5–7 min. Drizzle ranch, serve.

Oxtails

Prep + Cook 2 hr | Serves 6

Ingredients: 3 lbs oxtails, 2 tbsp oil, 1 onion, 3 cloves garlic, 2 cups beef broth, 2 carrots, 2 potatoes, salt, pepper, thyme.

Instructions: Brown oxtails in oil. Sauté onion & garlic. Add broth, carrots, potatoes, thyme. Simmer 1.5–2 hr until tender. Season, serve hot.

Key Lime Pie

Prep ~1 hr | Serves 8

Ingredients: 1 graham crust, 14 oz sweetened condensed milk, 1/2 cup key lime juice, 4 egg yolks, whipped cream, lime slices.

Instructions: Mix milk, juice, yolks. Pour in crust. Bake 10–12 min. Cool, chill 2 hr. Top with cream & lime.
